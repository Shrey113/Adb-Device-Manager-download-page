## üìÑ FAQ

<details>
  <summary><b>Do I need special drivers for my Android devices?</b></summary>
  Universal ADB drivers are included with our application. Some specific device models may require manufacturer-specific drivers for full functionality.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><b>Is root access required on my Android device?</b></summary>
  No, our app works perfectly with non-rooted devices for most features. Some advanced features work better with root access.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><b>How does this affect my device's battery life?</b></summary>
  Our companion app is highly optimized for minimal battery impact. You can configure power-saving settings to further reduce battery usage.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><b>Can I transfer files between my PC and Android devices?</b></summary>
  Yes, the app includes a powerful file manager that allows drag-and-drop file transfers between Windows and your Android devices.
</details>
