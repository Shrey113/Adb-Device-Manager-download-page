Installation Guide

Follow these simple steps to install and set up ADB Device Manager

Step 1: APK Installer

First, download and open the APK installer. This installer will set up the necessary files for "adb_devices.exe". You can delete the installer after installation is complete.

Step 2: Installation Dialog

When you click "Install APK", a notification will appear asking if you want to install this app. Click "Install" to proceed with the installation.

Step 3: Google Sign-In

To continue using the app, you'll need to sign in with your Google account. Your profile data will only be shared with the ADB Device Manager app and the secure local Windows tool.

Step 3 (continued): Account Access

After signing in, you'll see your Google account connected to the application. This allows the app to securely manage your device.

Step 4: App Permissions

Configure access to device features. The app requires certain permissions to properly manage your Android device. All permissions are necessary for full functionality.

Step 5: Background Service

The app will now set up a background service for adb_devices.exe. This service runs in the background even after closing the app, allowing for continuous device management and notification handling.

  • Runs in background even after closing the app
  • Sends notifications, calls, and media data to Windows
  • Controls music, messages, and phone events

Installation complete! You can now use ADB Device Manager to control your Android device from your Windows PC.
