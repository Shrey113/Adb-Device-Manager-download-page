Why choose us?

The ultimate tool for managing multiple Android devices from your Windows PC,perfect for developers, testers, and power users.

Multi-Device Management

Connect multiple Android devices simultaneously via USB, QR code, or wireless. Switch seamlessly between them with a single click.

Desktop Mode Experience

Transform your Android experience with our desktop mode. Run Android apps in Windows-like windows with full drag & drop support.

Complete Device Control

Take full control of your Android devices directly from your Windows PC with screen mirroring and real-time interaction.

Deep Windows Integration

Experience seamless Android-Windows integration with background services, startup support, and real-time notifications.

Media Control Center

Control all your Android media playback directly from Windows. Manage Spotify, YouTube, and other media apps without touching your device.

Real-Time Notification Center

View and respond to all Android notifications directly from your Windows desktop. Never miss an important message again.
